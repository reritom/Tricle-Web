Get Started

Once you have logged in, you'll be shown a form asking for three keys, the images you want to scramble, and whether you want to scramble or unscramble.

The order of your keys is important and must be remembered. The keys can be whatever you want, but try not to make them too obvious.

The keys are case-sensitive, should be at least three characters long, and shouldn't contain whitespace.

Select the bmp/jpeg/png images that you want to hide, select scramble, and click start.

Or if you want to unhide some images, select unscramble.

You'll be redirected to a download page. The download should start automatically, but if it doesn't, click the download button.

Once you have downloaded your processed files, click the blue button, which will remove any temporary files left on our servers.
